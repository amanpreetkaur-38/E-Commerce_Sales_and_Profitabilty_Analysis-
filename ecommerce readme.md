### E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard

##### 1\. Project Overview

This project presents an E-Commerce Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer contribution, product performance, and operational efficiency. The dashboard provides structured insights to support data-driven business decisions.



##### 2\. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Order Count: 6,452

Total Sales: 147.17M

Total Profit: 7.29M

Profit Margin: 4.96%

Profit per Order: 1.13K

Average Shipping Days: 3.49

Total Quantity Sold: 19K



#### 3\. Overall Financial Summary

Total Sales: 147,166,629.06

Total Profit: 7,294,517.94

Overall Profit Margin: 4.96%





##### 4\. Monthly Sales and Profit Trends

Monthly Sales range approximately between 10M and 14M.

Monthly Profit ranges approximately between 0.2M and 0.8M.

Sales and Profit are analyzed both by Order Month and by Calendar Month (January to December).
